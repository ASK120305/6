Lionel Messi

About Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. Often considered one of the greatest players of all time, he has won numerous awards, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or titles.

Career Highlights

  • 7-time Ballon d'Or winner
  • 4-time Champions League winner
  • 10-time La Liga champion
  • 2021 Copa America champion
  • 2022 FIFA World Cup champion
